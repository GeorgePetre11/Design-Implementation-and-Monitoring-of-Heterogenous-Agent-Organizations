## 4. Risk Assessment
### Regulatory Risks
- **Risk**: Compliance with Romanian labor laws and data protection regulations (GDPR).
- **Impact**: High (fines, legal penalties).
- **Mitigation**: Hire local legal advisors to ensure compliance.

### Market Risks
- **Risk**: Intense competition from established local players.
- **Impact**: Medium (reduced market share, slower growth).
- **Mitigation**: Differentiate through superior service quality and customer relationships.

### Operational Risks
- **Risk**: Cultural and language barriers.
- **Impact**: Medium (communication issues, integration problems).
- **Mitigation**: Hire bilingual staff and train on cultural nuances.

### Competitive Risks
- **Risk**: Losing clients to competitors with deeper market penetration.
- **Impact**: High (reduced market share, lower revenue).
- **Mitigation**: Develop a strong brand presence and customer loyalty programs.

## 5. Strategic Options & Recommendation
### Strategic Option 1: Gradual Entry
- **Approach**: Start with a small office and hire local staff.
- **Pros**: Lower initial costs, gradual adaptation to market.
- **Cons**: Slower market penetration, limited early revenue.

### Strategic Option 2: Rapid Expansion
- **Approach**: Open a larger office and hire more staff immediately.
- **Pros**: Faster market penetration, higher initial revenue.
- **Cons**: Higher initial costs, greater risk of operational inefficiencies.

### Strategic Option 3: Joint Venture
- **Approach**: Partner with a local IT services company.
- **Pros**: Immediate market presence, shared risk and resources.
- **Cons**: Loss of control, potential conflicts.

### Recommendation
- **Option**: Gradual Entry.
- **Justification**: Given the company's mid-size nature and limited budget, a gradual approach will ensure稳健的财务分析和风险管理，建议采用逐步进入市场的方式。这种策略有助于公司适应当地市场，同时降低初期成本和风险。通过这种方法，公司可以更好地了解客户需求，建立稳固的客户基础，并逐步扩大业务规模。
